# Moonlight Sonata — 3rd Movement (Play-by-Notes Grid)

Format designed for **beginners**: each table shows **beats as rows** and **measures as columns**.

**How to read a cell**:

- Notes are in play order, separated by spaces.

- Chords (play together) are like `[C#4 E4 G#4]`.

- If a cell has two lines, the top line is a **hold** (keep pressed) and the next line is what to **play** on that beat.

- `r` means rest.
