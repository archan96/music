## How to read this
- **Notes** are written in scientific pitch notation, e.g. `G#4`.
- Chords (press together) are wrapped like `[G#4 C#5 E5]`.
- **Beats** are positions *inside the measure* in quarter-note beats (0 → 4). Fractions like `7/4` mean 1.75 beats.
- **Dur** is a fraction of a **whole note**: `1/16` = sixteenth note, `1/8` = eighth, `1/4` = quarter, etc.
- `REST` appears when that hand is silent at that onset.
