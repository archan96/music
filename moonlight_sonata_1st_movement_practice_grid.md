## How to practise (super simple)
1. **LH alone**: just the bass note at the start of each bar, count 1–2–3–4 slowly.
2. **RH alone**: treat each beat as a triplet → play the 3 notes, then relax on the `—`.
3. Together: aim for **LH on beat 1** + RH triplets flowing evenly.
